About

Gene Dai is a product builder and Co-Founder & CPO of OpenJobs AI, an autonomous AI recruiting platform that reached #1 on Product Hunt. He previously held senior product leadership roles at BOSS Zhipin (BZ/2076.HK), China's leading mobile recruiting platform, Liepin (6100.HK), Zuoyebang, and Zhidemai Tech (300785.SZ), spanning recruiting, education, and e-commerce. He has published 272+ articles on AI and HRTech across Medium, Substack, and his personal blog, and maintains 133 open-source repositories on GitHub.

  • AI-Native Recruiting — Building Mira, an end-to-end autonomous AI recruiter that handles sourcing, screening, outreach, and interview scheduling.
  • Product Velocity — Led products at BOSS Zhipin, Liepin, Zuoyebang, and Zhidemai Tech. Deep experience in user growth, community, and platform products.
  • Domain Expertise — Head of Product at Kanzhun and BOSS Zhipin, Head of User Product at Liepin. Understanding hiring from every angle.
  • Builder & Writer — 272+ articles on AI, HRTech, and the people shaping the industry.
